Brownie Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • Sugar: 2 cups
  • Melted Butter: 1 cup
  • Eggs: 4
  • Cocoa Powder: 1/2 cup
  • Vanilla: 1 teaspoon
  • Salt: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Flour: 1 1/4 cups

Directions

  1. 1. Mix the Sugar and Melted butter together
  2. 2. Add the Eggs. Mix until it is emulsified
  3. 3. Add the salt, vanilla and cocoa powder. Mix
  4. 4. Mix in the flour
  5. Optional. Add a handful of chocolate chips to the mixture
  6. 5. Pour into a greased 9x13 dish. Bake at 350 for 28-30 minutes.
